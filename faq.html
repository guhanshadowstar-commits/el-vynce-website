Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, production, and care of your made-to-order EL VYNCE piece.

Every EL VYNCE piece is made to order. Production begins once your order has been placed, and orders are processed within 3–5 business days after payment confirmation.

Estimated delivery is 5–7 working days within India. Delivery timelines may vary depending on your location and the courier partner handling your shipment. Once your order ships, you will receive tracking information via email.

Orders cannot be cancelled once they have entered printing or production. If you need to cancel, please email elvynceofficial@gmail.com immediately — cancellation is only possible before your order enters processing.

Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at elvynceofficial@gmail.com with photos of the issue and your order receipt. Once verified, we will provide a free replacement. Cash refunds are never issued under any circumstance.

As each EL VYNCE piece is custom, printed-on-demand, and made to order, we do not offer returns, exchanges, or refunds once an order has been placed. This policy protects the quality, hygiene, and personalization integrity of every garment we produce. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

Each Drop has its own size chart with Drop-specific measurements. You can view the full chart from any product page by selecting "Size Chart" next to the size selector.

Payments are processed through secure third-party gateways (such as Razorpay). Your card or UPI details are never stored on EL VYNCE servers.
